Welcome to my very first GSoC 2026 blog post! This summer, I have the incredible opportunity to contribute to the **Accord Project**. 

## What I Achieved This Week

1. **Environment Setup**: Fully configured my local development environment for the `template-playground` and `template-engine` repositories.
2. **Community Bonding**: Met with my mentors to discuss our roadmap and set expectations for the upcoming weeks.
3. **Initial PRs**: Opened my first pull request addressing a small UI bug in the logic editor to get familiar with the CI/CD pipeline.

## Challenges

The biggest challenge this week was understanding the intricacies of the `TemplateArchiveProcessor`. There are a lot of moving parts in how logic is compiled and executed within a sandboxed environment. Thankfully, my mentors provided excellent guidance.

## Next Steps

Next week, I plan to dive deep into refactoring the template engine compilation step to support optional caching, which should significantly improve performance in the playground.

Stay tuned for more updates!
